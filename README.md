# Hotel Management System (HMS)

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/✨_Live_Demo-007BFF?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://lilvb.github.io/HMS_project/)

A modern, full-featured *web-based Hotel Management System* built to streamline every aspect of hotel operations—*from guest booking to checkout, housekeeping to analytics*—all in one intuitive platform.

Designed with *compliance, **scalability, and **user experience* in mind, HMS adheres to *GDPR, **PCI-DSS, and **Egyptian Data Protection Law* standards.

---

## Key Features

### Reservation Management  
Real-time booking with dynamic pricing, multi-room selection, promo codes, and instant confirmation. Intelligent inventory control prevents overbooking.

### Check-In & Check-Out  
Seamless guest processing with ID verification, digital signatures, auto room assignment, and express checkout.

### Housekeeping Operations  
Live room status tracking, automated task assignment, mobile updates for staff, and maintenance request logging—with performance metrics.

### Billing & Payments  
Automated folios, PCI-DSS compliant payments (credit card, PayPal, etc.), split billing, and tax-compliant invoices.

### Guest Management  
Full guest profiles, stay history, preference tracking, and loyalty program support for personalized service.

### Reports & Analytics  
Real-time dashboards for occupancy, revenue, ADR, RevPAR, and more. Export reports to *PDF, Excel, or CSV*.
